Parasite Diseases

Malaria

Affected Part: Destruction of RBC

Parasite: Plasmodium species

Symptoms: Fever with chills and sweating

Pyorrhea

Affected Part: Gums

Parasite: Entamoeba gingivalis

Symptoms: Gum bleeding and inflammation

Stone Disease

Affected Part: Urinary tract

Parasite: Trypanosoma

Symptoms: Pain and blood in urine

Amoebic Dysentery

Affected Part: Intestines

Parasite: Entamoeba histolytica

Symptoms: Diarrhea with blood and mucus

Kala-azar

Affected Part: Spleen and liver

Parasite: Leishmania donovani

Symptoms: Fever
