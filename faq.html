FAQ

Informational purposes only. Consult a healthcare professional for advice.

What does a fever mean?

A fever (above 100.4°F) often signals your body fighting an infection. Check our Symptom Checker for more symptoms. If severe, consult a doctor.

How can I tell if it’s just a cold?

Look for a runny nose, sneezing, and mild cough. Severe symptoms might indicate something else—use our tool to compare. Seek medical advice if unsure.

When should I worry about a headache?

If it’s sudden, severe, or with vision changes, seek help. Mild headaches can often be managed with rest—see our Health Tips. Consult a professional if persistent.

What’s shortness of breath a sign of?

It could be asthma, anxiety, or something serious like a heart issue. Check symptoms and consult a doctor if severe.

Can I trust the Symptom Checker?

It’s a guide, not a diagnosis. Always see a healthcare provider for accurate advice. Use it alongside our Personalized Health insights.
