FAQ

Common Health Questions

What does a fever mean? A fever (above 100.4°F) often signals your body fighting an infection. Check our Symptom Checker for more symptoms.

How can I tell if it’s just a cold? Look for a runny nose, sneezing, and mild cough. Severe symptoms might indicate something else—use our tool to compare.

When should I worry about a headache? If it’s sudden, severe, or with vision changes, seek help. Mild headaches can often be managed with rest—see our Health Tips.

What’s shortness of breath a sign of? It could be asthma, anxiety, or something serious like a heart issue. Check symptoms and consult a doctor if severe.

Can I trust the Symptom Checker? It’s a guide, not a diagnosis. Always see a healthcare provider for accurate advice.
