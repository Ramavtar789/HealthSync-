Health Tips

Informational purposes only. Consult a healthcare professional for advice.

How to Stay Hydrated

Drink 8-10 glasses of water daily. Carry a reusable bottle, eat water-rich foods like fruits, and sip consistently throughout the day. Avoid excessive caffeine.

Common Cold Remedies

Rest well, drink warm fluids like tea or soup, use honey for a sore throat, and try a humidifier to ease congestion. Over-the-counter meds can help too.

How to Stay Calm

Practice deep breathing: inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4, exhale for 4. Take short breaks, listen to music, or step outside for fresh air.

How to Handle a Headache

Drink water, rest in a quiet, dark room, apply a cold or warm compress, and avoid screens. If persistent, consider a mild pain reliever.

How to Stop Overthinking

Write down your thoughts to clear your mind, focus on one task at a time, practice mindfulness, or talk to a friend to shift perspective.
