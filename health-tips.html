Health Tips

Informational purposes only. Consult a healthcare professional for advice.

Headache

Rest in a dark, quiet room, drink plenty of water, apply a cold or warm compress to your forehead, and consider over-the-counter pain relief like ibuprofen if needed.

Abdominal Pain

Apply a warm compress to your stomach, avoid heavy or spicy meals, stay hydrated, and seek a doctor if the pain is severe or accompanied by fever.

Fever

Stay hydrated with water or electrolyte drinks, rest as much as possible, use a cool compress on your forehead, and consult a doctor if your temperature exceeds 103Â°F or lasts over 3 days.

Cough

Drink warm fluids like tea, use honey or throat lozenges, avoid smoke or dust, and rest your voice; see a doctor if it persists beyond 2 weeks.

Sore Throat

Gargle with warm salt water, drink tea with honey, rest your voice, and avoid smoking; seek medical help if severe or with fever.

Nausea

Sip ginger tea or clear fluids, eat small amounts of bland food (e.g., crackers), rest, and avoid strong smells; seek help if vomiting lasts over 24 hours.

Fatigue

Get 7-8 hours of sleep, eat a balanced diet with iron-rich foods, reduce stress with light exercise or meditation, and consult a doctor if chronic.

Stomach Ache

Use a heating pad on your abdomen, eat bland foods like rice or bananas, avoid spicy or fatty foods, and stay hydrated.

Diarrhea

Rehydrate with water or oral rehydration solutions, eat bland foods like bananas or rice, avoid sugary drinks or dairy, and see a doctor if lasting over 2 days.

Back Pain

Stretch gently, apply heat or ice packs, maintain good posture, avoid heavy lifting, and consult a doctor if pain persists.

Dizziness

Sit or lie down immediately, drink water to hydrate, avoid sudden movements, eat small frequent meals, and seek help if frequent or severe.
